Our Story

Fraudulink was born from personal experience. Every member of our team has witnessed someone they love—a grandparent, parent, or family friend—receive suspicious phone calls that left them confused, frightened, or financially harmed.

We came together at SheHacks 2026 with a shared mission: to use our technical skills to create a tool that could help protect those who are most vulnerable to predatory scam tactics. We believe everyone deserves to feel safe when answering their phone.
