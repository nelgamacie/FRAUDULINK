The Inspiration

Fraudulink was born from personal experience. Every member of our team has witnessed someone they love—a grandparent, parent, or family friend—receive suspicious phone calls that left them confused, frightened, or financially devastated.

We watched helplessly as scammers used increasingly sophisticated tactics to prey on the trust and goodwill of seniors. The heartbreak of seeing someone lose their life savings or feel violated after falling victim to a scam is something we never want another family to experience.

That's why we came together at SheHacks 2026—to transform our frustration into action and build technology that genuinely protects the people who need it most.

Why Seniors Are Targeted

Trust & Politeness

Raised to be courteous and trusting, making them less likely to hang up or question authority figures on calls.

Financial Stability

Built up retirement savings, home equity, and good credit over decades—making them lucrative targets.

Social Isolation

Living alone, they may welcome phone contact, making them more vulnerable to friendly-sounding manipulation.

Language Barriers

Immigrant seniors face additional challenges, often targeted with scams in their native language.
